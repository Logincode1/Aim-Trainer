Welcome to the Aim Trainer! The goal of this game is to click on the targets that appear on the screen as quickly as possible. You can choose a duration for the game, and your score will be based on how many targets you hit within that time.

To start the game, select a duration from the dropdown menu and click the "Start" button. The timer will begin, and targets will appear randomly on the canvas. Click on the targets to score points. When the time is up, your final score will be displayed.
